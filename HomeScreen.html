Road Trip Pals

We seek to provide services for inter-city travelers. The main purpose of our company is to encourage car sharing and improve the connection between people in the world. We allow customers to travel inter-city with a low cost under full security.

Simplified Traveling

We seek to simplify the roadtrip experience by connecting people within the same community with similar or equal departure and destination points as long as the journey is more than 30 minutes.

No money for traveling? No problem
